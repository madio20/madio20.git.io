About Me

Here is where you learn all about me, my interests, and what I am confident in.

Note: This may or may not be completely updated all the time

Hello! My name is Madison Piehler.

I am a hardworking graphic design student at Assumption University with a double music major. I am also an accomplished musician in flute and alto saxophone. My colleagues would describe me as smart, kind, and determined. I believe that music and art are keys to self-expression which is important to everyday life. My goal is to make people smile and inspire them to do what they wish.

I am interested in Music and Art. I am proficient in the flute and alto sax and started playing instruments in 4th grade. I was in many band groups starting in middle school and through college. After transferring to Assumption University, I started pursuing art and graphic design in college. I had to work exceptionally hard in those fields because I took no art or design classes in high school. I also like videogames and tabletop games like DND.
